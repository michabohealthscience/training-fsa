A note on Quality assessments

We will skip in our training today! Assessing the technical quality of the metabolomics assay is not the focus in this tutorial we will not delve any further into quality assessments. However, the trainees should just be aware that the metabolomics data we are using has been assessed at a technical level using quality control samples and passes accetable criteria.

Whenever using omics datasets, sufficient quality assessments need to be performed to assess the quality of the data from a tehcnical perspective.

These are specific based on the Omics and technology used. Details for quality assessments in Metabolomics can be found xxx. Details for quality assessments in Transcriptomics can be found xxx.

Quality assessments was performed on all 6 of the participating ring trial labs. All but one passed the quality assessments.

One of the key metrics involved in this assessment is to evaluate intrastudy quality control-samples which are a pooled sample derived from all other samples. The intrastudy QC samples were assessed using Principal Component Analysis (PCA) and assessing the medium relative standard deviation across all features. Following this evaulation the data from RP8 was deemed not be sufficient high quality and so was removed from further analysis.
